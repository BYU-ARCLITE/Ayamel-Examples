How to add this content to an LMS.

You can only add this content via LIT. You need three things to set it up: a launch url, a consumer key, and a consumer secret. The last two items are sometimes referred to as the public and private keys. For this content, these items are the following:

  • Launch URL: @routes.ContentController.viewLms(content.id.get, course.get.id.get).absoluteURL()
  • Consumer Key: @course.get.id.get
  • Consumer Secret: @course.get.lmsKey
