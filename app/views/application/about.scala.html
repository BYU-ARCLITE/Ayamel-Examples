About Ayamel

Ayamel is a web-based system for streaming video and audio content especially as it applies to learning a foreign language. Additional aids are available to be used in conjuction with the media, such as captions and annotations. A key feature of the site enables learners to directly interact with subtitle tracks to quickly access translations for foreign words and phrases and view relevant annotations. The translations are taken from our own dictionary web service, WordReference.com translation API, Merriam-Webster Dictionary/Translation APIs, and the Google Translate API. Teachers or course designers are able to aggregate media into a single location and set up specific conditions in order to optimize the learning experience.
